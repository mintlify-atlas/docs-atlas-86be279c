## Overview

The About page (`about.astro`) provides comprehensive information about Lake Ozark Christian Church's mission, values, and denominational affiliation with the Christian Church (Disciples of Christ).

**File location**: `src/pages/about.astro`

## Page structure

### Hero section

Full-width hero with:
- Background image of church sanctuary
- Mission statement: "Dedicated to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ"
- CTA buttons for worship and staff pages


### Our story section

**Content highlights:**
- Faith as a way of life
